I'M GATWIRI

I'M GATWIRI is a fashion brand owned and founded by the renowned fashion stylist Emmaculate Gatwiri.



This brand was started in the year 2022 after she had a successful career as a fashion stylist. She was recognized by many because of her amazing work and got to work with famous celebrities and renowned politicians. She is a professional stylist who has worked with a lot of people in styling them for all kinds of events. She then decided to start her own brand which she named after herself.

This brand is now one of the best fashion brands in this country. This brand focuses on styling for all kinds of events. This brand being a five star rated assures you of nothing but the best.
